About Me

I'm a second year student bachelor of science in Computer Science and Cognitive & Brain Science at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. I speak Python, Java, Javascript,HTML/HTML5 and R very well.
I've enjoyed the courses of Object Oriented Programming, Data Structures, Logics, Machine Learning and Algorithms, oh and I also really liked Neuroanatomy :)
I currently work in the Social Cognition Lab at HUJI, as the software developer of the lab. I'm writing all lab's web-experiments in JS and its data analysis in Python and use a lot of Pandas, Numpy and Matplotlib.
Over the past year I was an instructor at QueenB, a women-empowerment organization.
I taught a group of ten 15-year-old girls and that was very meaningful to me.
I used to be a F-16 Flight Simulator Instructor in the Israeli Air Force.
I enjoy tracking, diving, photography and yoga
